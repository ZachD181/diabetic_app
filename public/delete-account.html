Insulin Daily

Delete Account and Data Request

This page explains how a user can request deletion of an Insulin Daily account and associated saved data.

What can be deleted

Account profile Registered account details such as name, email, linked clinician details, and password-based sign-in records.
Saved app records Stored blood sugar logs, chart snapshots, wearable sync records, emergency contacts, recommendations, and message history tied to the account.
Local browser data Guest-mode and browser-stored records can also be cleared directly from the device or browser that saved them.

How to request deletion

Step 1 Use the app’s support contact path and clearly state that you are requesting account and associated data deletion for Insulin Daily.
Step 2 Include the account email address connected to the app so the request can be matched to the correct record.
Step 3 If you used guest mode only, clear the browser or device storage on the same device to remove locally saved entries.

Current deletion path

At this stage, deletion requests are handled through the app’s support workflow rather than a self-service in-app delete button. Support information is available at /support.html.

When making a request, specify that you want your account and associated stored data removed from Insulin Daily.

Important notes

This page describes the request path for account deletion. It is not a live emergency or medical support channel.
Some operational or provider-side records may need limited retention where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal obligations.
