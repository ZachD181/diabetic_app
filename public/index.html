Safety notes

Use caution with any bolus/fast acting estimate

Do not rely on this app alone for severe highs, lows, ketones, vomiting, illness, or pump or CGM alarms.
Insulin on board logic differs by person and device. Confirm with your care team how much correction insulin should be subtracted.
This prototype is not a production HIPAA deployment and would need secure hosting, audit logging, encryption, and legal review before real clinical use.
